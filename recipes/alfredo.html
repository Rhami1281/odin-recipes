Creamy Alfredo

This recipe is incredible simple and quick and will yield an impressive dish to impress anyone!

Ingredients

  • One pound of noodles
  • four TBS salted butter
  • 1/4 cup grated parmasean cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

How to make Alfredo

  1. boil noodles
  2. melt butter in pan on low heat
  3. transfer noodles to melted butter
  4. mix together until noodles are covered
  5. add parmasean cheese and stir
  6. add salt and pepper
